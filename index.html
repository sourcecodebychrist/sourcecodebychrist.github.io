About SOURCECODE

Previous Next

SourceCode is the annual tech-fest in which young minds participate in various technical and non-technical events. It's primary goal is to provide the students with a platform to inculcate a culture of thinking, innovation and creativity by understanding the current problems in our society and overcoming them. The event is run and managed by CUESTIC which also conducts several awareness activities under ME-AW (Mobile radiation and E-waste Awareness Week).

SourceCode is into its 7th year and this year it's all set with a wider variety of very interesting events for the young minds all around the world!We look forward for your enthusiastic participation.
